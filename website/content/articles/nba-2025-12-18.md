**Chicago Bulls 127 - 111 Cleveland Cavaliers**  
The Chicago Bulls secured a convincing 127-111 victory over the Cleveland Cavaliers, with standout performances from Josh Giddey and Coby White. Giddey achieved his sixth triple-double of the season, scoring 23 points with 11 rebounds and 11 assists, and matched his career high by sinking five three-pointers. Coby White contributed significantly with 25 points, while Nikola Vucevic added 20 points and nine rebounds. The Bulls, who shot an impressive 56.2%, managed to break a losing streak against the Cavaliers, having lost 12 of their last 13 encounters with them. Despite Donovan Mitchell's 32-point effort for Cleveland, the Cavaliers continued to struggle, recording their seventh loss in the past 10 games. Notably, Ayo Dosunmu of the Bulls missed his third consecutive game due to a sprained thumb. The teams are set to face off again in Cleveland on Friday.

**Minnesota Timberwolves 110 - 116 Memphis Grizzlies**  
Jaren Jackson Jr. led the Memphis Grizzlies to a 116-110 victory over the Minnesota Timberwolves with an impressive 28 points and 12 rebounds on Wednesday night. Jock Landale contributed significantly with 20 points and 10 rebounds, including a career-high four 3-pointers, as the Grizzlies secured their win with key plays in the final minutes. Despite missing their top scorer, Anthony Edwards, the Timberwolves managed a strong performance with Julius Randle scoring 21 points and Rudy Gobert adding 16 points and 16 rebounds. The game saw Minnesota losing for only the second time in nine games. Notably, Memphis was without star player Ja Morant due to a left ankle sprain and Cam Spencer for personal reasons. Brandon Clarke returned for Memphis, scoring six points in his first game since March. The Grizzlies will host Washington next, while the Timberwolves are set to face Oklahoma City.
