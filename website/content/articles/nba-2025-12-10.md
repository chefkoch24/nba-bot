**Orlando Magic 117 - 108 Miami Heat**  
The Orlando Magic secured a 117-108 victory over the Miami Heat, advancing to the NBA Cup semifinals, driven by Desmond Bane's stellar performance matching his season high with 37 points. Despite Miami's early 15-0 lead fueled by Tyler Herro, the Magic staged a comeback, with Bane's critical scoring in the fourth quarter establishing a solid lead. Jalen Suggs and Paolo Banchero supported with 20 and 18 points respectively. The Heat's struggle from beyond the arc was evident as they hit just 24.2% of their 3-pointers, with Herro missing all six attempts. Notably, this game marked the Magic's first of the season without Franz Wagner due to a high ankle sprain. Miami, led by Norman Powell with 21 points, has now faced three defeats in Orlando this season. The Magic now move on to face the New York Knicks in Las Vegas, while the Heat prepare to host Toronto.
